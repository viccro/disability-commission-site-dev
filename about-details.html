The Arlington Disability Commission works to improve access, inclusion, and equity for people with disabilities in Arlington.

What We Focus On

  • Advising town government on disability-related policy and decisions.
  • Identifying barriers in facilities, programs, communication, and services.
  • Partnering with departments and community groups to improve accessibility.

How To Engage

  • Attend public meetings and share feedback.
  • Send us specific accessibility concerns through the contact page.
  • Subscribe to updates to stay informed about commission work.

For day-to-day updates and announcements, visit the Meetings and Updates pages in the main navigation.
