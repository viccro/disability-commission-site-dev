If you need an accommodation to participate in a meeting, event, program, or Town service, use this page to start the request process.

What You Can Request

Accommodation requests may include communication access, mobility access, digital access, language support, or other adjustments that allow you to participate fully. If you are not sure what to ask for, describe the barrier you are facing and we will help identify the next step.

How to Submit a Request

The best first step is to contact the Commission or the Town through the contact page with the details of your request. Please make requests as early as possible so there is time to coordinate logistics, vendors, equipment, or meeting adjustments when needed.

Include These Details

  • The meeting, event, program, or service you want to access
  • The date, time, and location, if known
  • The accommodation you are requesting
  • The best way to contact you with follow-up questions

For Upcoming Meetings and Events

If your request is related to a public meeting or Commission event, include the event name and date in your message. This helps staff and volunteers route the request quickly and confirm what arrangements are already in place.

If You Encountered a Barrier

If you were unable to access a program, service, or public space because an accommodation was not available, you can also report the incident so the issue is documented and reviewed.
