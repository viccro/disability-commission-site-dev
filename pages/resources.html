Civic Life in Arlington

  • Arlington Disability Commission's Meeting Calendar and Agendas and Minutes
  • Accommodations for municipal meetings (such as CART or interpreters): email DisabilityComm@town.arlington.ma.us, or call 781-316-3170
  • Arlington Disability Commissioner Description
  • 2025 Commission Brochure
  • Arlington Community Media, Inc. (ACMI) is the local community access channel, which is available on cable and on the web. ACMI's Government Channel includes many municipal meetings, such as the Select Board, Town Meeting, and School Committee, among others. ACMI programming is closed-captioned on YouTube.
  • Arlington's ADA Self-Evaluation and Transition Plan (418 pages, published November 2019). Under Title II of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), the Town of Arlington was required to complete this self-evaluation of the Town's public facilities, programs, services, activities and events to determine compliance with the ADA and any barriers to accessibility. This document also contains a Transition Plan to prioritize the identified barriers and how they will be corrected.
