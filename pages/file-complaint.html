Have you experienced an accessibility barrier or incident? Use this page to report an issue or file a formal complaint.

Americans with Disabilities Act Grievance Procedure

This grievance procedure is established to meet the requirements of the ADA. It may be used by anyone who wishes to file a complaint alleging discrimination on the basis of disability in the provision of services, activities, programs, or benefits by the Town of Arlington.

The complaint should be submitted as soon as possible, preferably within 60 calendar days of the alleged violation to:
