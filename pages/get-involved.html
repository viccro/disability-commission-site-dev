Attend a Meeting

Our monthly Disability Commission meetings are open to all interested residents, and we strongly encourage you to stop by!

When?

The Commission meets on the third Wednesday of each month, from 5:30-7 PM. We post upcoming meetings, agendas, and any schedule changes on our official Town webpage.

Where?

Our meetings are run in a hybrid format, to make it easier for residents to participate. You can join us:

  • in person in the 2nd Floor HHS Conference Room (2nd Floor), 27 Maple Street. Registration is not required for in-person attendance.
  • virtually via Zoom, using the meeting link provided in each meeting's agenda. Registration is required for virtual attendance.

How?

If you need an accommodation to participate in this meeting, please contact dei@town.arlington.ma.us as early as possible. More details may be found on our Request Accommodations page.

Join the Disability Commission!

The Disability Commission is looking for new volunteers. We advocate for the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities in our community. Our work includes researching local challenges, advising town leaders on disability laws, recommending improvements to policies and services, and organizing events and programs on disability issues. We partner closely with the Town's accessibility coordinator to help ensure equal access for all residents with disabilities.

We're looking for volunteers from diverse backgrounds and experiences. The time commitment is at least 8 hours per month, including monthly meetings held in the evening on the third Wednesday and one subcommittee meeting per month. We especially encourage people with disabilities to apply, though anyone with relevant experience or strong interest in disability issues is welcome. To learn more about our work, check out our our Mission and Scope and Updates pages or come to a meeting.

To apply to be a Commissioner, email your resume and letter of interest to TownManager@town.arlington.ma.us, or send it by mail to

Town Manager
730 Massachusetts Ave.
Arlington, MA 02476

For questions or more information, contact DisabilityComm@town.arlington.ma.us.
