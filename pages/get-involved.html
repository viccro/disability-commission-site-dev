Attend a Meeting

Our monthly Disability Commission meetings are open to all interested residents, and we strongly encourage you to stop by!

When?

The Commission meets on the third Wednesday of each month, from 5:30-7 PM. We post upcoming meetings, agendas, and any schedule changes on our official Town webpage.

Where?

Our meetings are run in a hybrid format, to make it easier for residents to participate. You can join us:

  • in person in the 2nd Floor HHS Conference Room (2nd Floor), 27 Maple Street. Registration is not required for in-person attendance.
  • virtually via Zoom, using the meeting link provided at the top of the meeting's agenda. Registration is required for virtual attendance.

How?

If you need an accommodation to participate in this meeting, please contact dei@town.arlington.ma.us as early as possible. More details may be found here.

Volunteer

Join the Disability Commission!

The Disability Commission is looking to fill several vacancies. The Commission advocates for the rights and full inclusion of people with disabilities in all aspects of community life. We research local challenges, advise local officials on compliance with disability laws, and recommend improvements to policies, services, and facilities. We also organize events and programs focused on disability-related issues. The Commission works closely with the Town's ADA coordinator to provide referrals, guidance, and technical assistance to ensure equal access for residents with physical, sensory, cognitive, and other disabilities.

The Commission relies on a diverse pool of volunteer Commissioners. Time commitment is at least 8 hours per month, which includes attending meetings typically the third Wednesday of each month between 5:30pm - 7pm, and participating in at least one subcommittee meeting once a month. Applicants with disabilities will be given priority, but all residents with relevant experience or strong interest in disability issues are encouraged to apply. To learn more about our work, please visit the Disability Commission's website.

To apply to be a Commissioner, email your resume and letter of interest to TownManager@town.arlington.ma.us, or send it to the Town Manager, 730 Massachusetts Ave., Arlington, MA 02476. For more information or questions, email DisabilityComm@town.arlington.ma.us.

Other Volunteer Opportunities

In addition to joining the Commission, we may have other volunteer opportunities coming down the road. If you're interested in joining our list to be notified of these chances to help out, please email DisabilityComm@town.arlington.ma.us to be added to the volunteer notification list.
